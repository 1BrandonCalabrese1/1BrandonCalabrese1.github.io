Prepare to be captivated by the extraordinary realm of Dungeons in SkyBlock. With its immersive blend of perilous obstacles and enticing treasures, Dungeons offers an unparalleled adventure that will leave you breathless.

At the heart of this captivating experience lies the Catacombs, the sole dungeon currently available. Whether you enter through the Catacombs Entrance or the Dungeon Hub, you'll find yourself embarking on a journey filled with mystery and excitement.

The dungeon map reveals a series of unique locations, each presenting its own set of trials and rewards. Begin your expedition in the Green Room, the starting point of every adventure. As you progress, the Brown Rooms will test your skills as you combat formidable mobs and seek out hidden Secrets.

Prepare for intense showdowns in the Mini-Boss Rooms, denoted by their striking Yellow color. Here, you'll face off against Mini-Bosses of exceptional strength, putting your abilities to the ultimate test. And don't forget the challenging Puzzle Rooms, represented by their enigmatic Purple hue. With each floor, the puzzles become progressively more intricate, offering a true test of wit and ingenuity.

Venture further, and you'll encounter the treacherous Orange Rooms. Exclusive to Floors 3 and above, these rooms are riddled with traps that demand nimbleness and precision to overcome. Clear the traps, find the secrets, and emerge victorious.
